How to Use IconWeb

1

Browse Sample Icons

Swipe or tap arrows in the preview carousel to view sample icons and see effects applied live.

2

Select Effects

Tap effects to toggle them on. Selected effects glow blue. Those effects are layered on preview.

3

Adjust Corner Radius

Click Settings to adjust the corner radius from 0 (square) to 300 (very rounded). Changes apply to preview and export.

4

Upload Your Icons

Drop PNGs/SVGs, or tap Browse.

5

Download

Tap the blue button bottom-right. We'll generate all variants and give you a ZIP.
