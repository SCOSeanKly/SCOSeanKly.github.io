Privacy Policy

Last updated: Mon 22 Sep 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile apps, websites, and services published by Show Creative (“we,” “our,” or “us”). We build privacy-respectful products and collect only the information necessary for each feature to work. Some of our apps collect no personal data at all; if a specific app needs data (for example, a photo you upload to generate an avatar), that collection is limited to the feature you choose to use.

Where to find face/photo data details (for App Review):
See Information We May Collect → User-Provided Content (Photos & Face Data), How We Use Information, Sharing & Disclosure, and Data Retention & Deletion.

1) Information We May Collect

Depending on which of our apps or features you use, we may collect:

A. User-Provided Content (Photos & Face Data)

  • If you choose to upload an image (e.g., to create an AI avatar), we process the photo you provide.
  • We do not perform facial recognition or store biometric identifiers. We do not extract facial landmarks for identification or authentication.
  • Photos are used only to deliver the feature you requested.

B. Basic App Diagnostics & Analytics (if present)

  • Some apps may collect limited, aggregated analytics (e.g., crash logs, performance metrics) to improve stability and user experience.
  • Where used, such analytics do not include the contents of your photos.

C. Optional Contact Information

  • If you email us for support, we may receive your email address and any information you include.

2) How We Use Information

  • To operate the specific feature you selected (e.g., generating an avatar from your uploaded photo).
  • To maintain and improve app performance, security, and reliability (diagnostics/analytics, where enabled).
  • To respond to support requests you initiate.

Photos & Face Data: When an app offers AI image generation, your photo is transmitted once—over an encrypted connection—to the selected image-generation provider to fulfill your request. We do not use your images for training, advertising, or any other secondary purpose.

3) Sharing & Disclosure

  • We never sell personal data.
  • For features like AI image generation, your photo may be shared only with the chosen third-party processor (e.g., OpenAI or Google Gemini) for one-time processing to deliver the result to you.
  • We may use service providers for hosting, delivery, diagnostics, or support. They act on our instructions and are bound by appropriate confidentiality and data-processing terms.

4) Data Retention & Deletion

  • Uploaded photos (including images that may contain faces) are automatically deleted from our servers and temporary caches within 24 hours or sooner after processing, unless a specific app explicitly states a different timeline in-app.
  • Diagnostics/analytics (if present) are retained only as needed to maintain and improve app stability.
  • Support emails are kept only as long as necessary to address your inquiry and for reasonable record-keeping.

5) Website Analytics

Our public website may use basic analytics to measure aggregate traffic and performance. This does not involve the contents of your photos. You can adjust cookie/analytics preferences via your browser settings.

6) Children’s Privacy

Our apps and services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7) Your Rights & Choices

You may request access, correction, or deletion of personal information we hold about you by contacting us at showcreative@icloud.com. For app data that resides only on your device, you can delete it by uninstalling the app.

8) Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the new version on this page and update the “Last updated” date.

9) Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: showcreative@icloud.com
